Choose Your Homebrew Method!

You can install both Aroma and Tiramisu at the same time and swap between the two!

Aroma

Pros:

  • Most up-to-date
  • WHUB Support
  • Pretendo Network

Cons:

  • No legacy homebrew (.elf)
  • No Homebrew Launcher Channel
  • Still a work in progress/beta

Continue to Aroma!

Tiramisu

Pros:

  • Homebrew Launcher Channel
  • Most homebrew
  • Not in beta

Cons:

  • No WHUBS
  • Most homebrew has moved to Aroma

Continue to Tiramisu!

Legacy Methods

These methods are no longer supported. Continue at your own risk!

  • HaxChi
  • ColdBoot HaxChi (CBHC)
  • Mocha
